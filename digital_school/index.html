Our Formations

Basics of Ecology

Learn the foundational concepts of ecosystems, biodiversity, and environmental interactions.

Formation 2: Human Anatomy

Explore the structure and functions of the human body in this introductory course.

Formation 3: Plant Physiology

Understand how plants grow, develop, and adapt to their environment.

Formation 4: Environmental Chemistry

Delve into the chemical processes and pollutants affecting our environment.

Formation 5: Zoology Fundamentals

Study the classification, behavior, and habitats of animals in this course.

Formation 6: Genetics and Heredity

Discover the principles of inheritance and the role of DNA in living organisms.

Formation 7: Marine Biology

Learn about life in oceans, seas, and the amazing organisms that inhabit them.

Formation 8: Microbiology Basics

Examine the microscopic organisms that influence life on Earth, from bacteria to viruses.

Trace the evolutionary history of life and understand the mechanisms of evolution.

Formation 10: Climate Change Science

Explore the science behind climate change and its impact on ecosystems and life.
